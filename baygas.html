BAY GAS

Established in 2014, Bay Gas Petroleum Distribution, Inc. is engaged in the refilling, retail, and, distribution of fuel and petroleum products in Cebu City.

Learn More 







About BAY GAS

Located in Brgy, Cansaga, Consolacion, Cebu, the station is an essential part of the transport industry in the area. Holding a dominant market share in the community, BGPDI provides fuel and other petroleum products retail services not only to companies, private vehicle owners, and public utility transport operators but also to government agencies and LGUs.

    The establishment is under the umbrella of MHR Properties Conglomerate, Inc. With six (6) local staff directly employed in the station.

Years after its successful operation, the company is now venturing into an innovative business venture that will help in fostering economic development in remote areas and will be a great way to support the government in its goal to eliminate the unsafe bote-bote system that operates as an underground economy.
